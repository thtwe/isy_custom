Send Multiple Payslip By Email

This app allows to send bulk salary slip by email , if HR wants to send salary reciept reports in bulk these module does that

key features:

  • HR Officer/Manager can send payslip of employee to their registered email with attachment of payslip.
  • HR Officer/Manager can also send bulk payslip of particular employee.

How to use

Employee Payslip Send By Email

HR Officer/Manager can send payslip of employee to his/her email by "Send By Mail" button on payslip form.


Email Template With Attachment For Payslip


Payslip Email

You can see the email with attachment of payslip



Send Multiple Payslip

You can send multiple payslip by email with attachment of payslip


Send Multiple Wizard

Confirmation wizard before sending multiple payslip


Display payslips in email
